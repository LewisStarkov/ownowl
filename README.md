## Claim one

**1. Star this repository.** &nbsp;That is the whole price.

**2. Sign in with GitHub** &nbsp;at [ownowl.app](https://ownowl.app).

**3. Pick a name.** &nbsp;Choose A or CNAME, enter the value, done. Live in seconds.

<br>

## Rules

**One per account.** &nbsp;Permanent. Names are never recycled.

**It lives with your star.** &nbsp;Re-checked daily. Unstar and the record is removed.

**No edits.** &nbsp;Unstar to release the name.

**3 to 63 characters.** &nbsp;Lowercase letters, digits and hyphens.

**CNAME targets must resolve.** &nbsp;Pointing at a host nobody owns is a subdomain takeover waiting to happen.

**Public IPv4 only.** &nbsp;No private, loopback or link-local ranges.

**Nothing harmful.** &nbsp;No phishing, malware or illegal content.
